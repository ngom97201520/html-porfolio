My top 3 movies of all-time.

Return of the Dragon

The Way of the Dragon (Chinese movie, originally released in the United States as Return of the Dragon) is a 1972 Hong Kong martial arts action comedy film written, co-produced and directed by Bruce Lee.

Flying Dagger

Flying Dagger (Chinese movie - Nervous Knife and Apsara Flying Cat) is a 1993 wuxia comedy film directed by Kevin Chu and written and produced by Wong Jing.

Titanic

Titanic was a British passenger liner, operated by the White Star Line, which sank in the North Atlantic Ocean on 15 April 1912 after striking an iceberg during her maiden voyage from Southampton, England, to New York City, United States.
